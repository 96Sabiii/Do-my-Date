Help

This calender helps you to memorize all your relevant tasks and stories.

Just click on a date to create an entry. If you want to view your entries in a list click on To-Do.

To clear all your entries from the storage click the trash can button in the navigation bar.

Your possibilities to create an entry are:

  • - Give your entry a headline
  • - Choose a color for the headline
  • - Add text to your canvas
  • - Edit the text with different fonts and colors
  • - Add picture links
  • - Duplicate the selected element
  • - Delete the selected element
  • - Save or cancel your entry
